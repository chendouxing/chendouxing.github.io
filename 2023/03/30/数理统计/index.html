记为

X∽B(n,P)X\backsim B(n,P) X∽B(n,P)

E(X)=n×PD(X)=n×P×(1−P)E(X)=n \times P\\ D(X)=n \times P \times (1-P) E(X)=n×PD(X)=n×P×(1−P)
